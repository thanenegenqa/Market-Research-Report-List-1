<p><p>The silicone oil market is categorized primarily into straight silicone oil and modified silicone oil. Straight silicone oil, characterized by its high stability and low viscosity, is widely utilized in applications such as lubricants and personal care products. Modified silicone oil, on the other hand, incorporates various additives to enhance properties like tensile strength and chemical resistance, making it suitable for specialized applications in textiles, automotive, and construction sectors. Together, they address diverse industrial needs while enhancing performance characteristics.</p></p>
