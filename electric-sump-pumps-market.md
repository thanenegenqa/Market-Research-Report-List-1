<p><ul><li>Submersible Pumps</li><li>Pedestal Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric sump pump market primarily features two types: submersible pumps and pedestal pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to operate underwater, effectively pumping out water from basements and crawl spaces. They are typically more powerful and quieter. In contrast, pedestal pumps have a motor mounted above the water level, making them easier to service but less effective in deep sump applications. Both types are essential for managing excess water and preventing flooding in residential and commercial properties.</p></p>
