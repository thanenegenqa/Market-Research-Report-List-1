<p><p>The Human Embryonic Stem Cells (HESC) market comprises three key types: totipotent, pluripotent, and unipotent stem cells. Totipotent stem cells can differentiate into any cell type, including placental cells, making them highly versatile. Pluripotent stem cells can develop into nearly all cell types but cannot form an entire organism. Unipotent stem cells are limited to differentiating into a specific cell type, offering targeted therapeutic applications. Each type has distinct potentials in research, regenerative medicine, and potential treatments.</p></p>
