<p><p>The Supercritical Boiler Market is primarily categorized into two types: Pure DC Supercritical Boilers and Compound Cycle Supercritical Boilers. Pure DC Supercritical Boilers operate at high pressures and temperatures, utilizing water above its critical point to enhance thermal efficiency and reduce emissions. In contrast, Compound Cycle Supercritical Boilers combine gas and steam cycles, optimizing energy generation and performance. Both types aim to improve efficiency in power generation while minimizing environmental impact, catering to growing energy demands and sustainability goals.</p></p>
