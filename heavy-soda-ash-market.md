<p><strong>The Heavy Soda Ash Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvay Method</li><li>Trona Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy soda ash market is primarily divided into two production methods: the Solvay Method and the Trona Method. The Solvay Method is a synthetic process that involves reacting sodium chloride with ammonia and carbon dioxide, producing high-purity soda ash. In contrast, the Trona Method extracts soda ash from natural deposits of trona ore, which is a combination of sodium carbonate, sodium bicarbonate, and water. Both methods cater to various industrial applications such as glass manufacturing and chemical production.</p></p>
