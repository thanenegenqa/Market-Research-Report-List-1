<p><p>The Maritime Thermal Imagers market consists of two main types: uncooled and cooled thermal imagers. Uncooled thermal imagers operate at ambient temperatures, using materials that provide thermal sensitivity without the need for cooling, making them cost-effective and lightweight. In contrast, cooled thermal imagers utilize cryogenic cooling systems to enhance their sensitivity and imaging capabilities, offering superior performance in detecting distant objects in low visibility conditions. Both types are essential for maritime surveillance, search and rescue, and navigation support.</p></p>
