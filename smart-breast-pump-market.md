<p><p>The smart breast pump market features two main types: electric single pumps and electric double pumps. Electric single pumps are designed to express milk from one breast at a time, offering portability and convenience for on-the-go mothers. In contrast, electric double pumps facilitate simultaneous milk extraction from both breasts, significantly reducing pumping time and enhancing milk supply efficiency. Both types often incorporate smart technology, allowing users to track pumping sessions, manage settings via apps, and receive personalized insights for improved breastfeeding experiences.</p></p>
