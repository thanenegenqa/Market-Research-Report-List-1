<p><p>Floating anchors are devices used to secure various structures in marine environments. There are several market types for floating anchors: </p><p>1. **Flat Anchors**: These have a low profile and are designed for stability on soft or muddy seabeds.</p><p>2. **Grapnel Anchors**: Featuring multiple prongs, they grip the seabed effectively and are used in rocky substrates.</p><p>3. **Floating Anchors**: Equipped with buoyancy, they maintain position above the seabed while anchoring.</p><p>4. **Other Anchors**: This category includes specialized designs tailored for unique marine conditions or requirements.</p></p>
