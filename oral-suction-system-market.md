<p><p>The Oral Suction System market is categorized into two primary types: Dry Smoking and Wet Pumping. Dry Smoking utilizes a suction mechanism that operates without the use of fluids, making it ideal for quick and efficient oral care procedures, particularly in emergency settings. In contrast, Wet Pumping incorporates fluids to enhance the suction process, offering a more thorough cleaning method often used in clinical and dental environments. Both systems cater to diverse needs in healthcare, improving patient outcomes and hygiene practices.</p></p>
