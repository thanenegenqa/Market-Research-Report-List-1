<p><p>Engineered wood products (EWPs) are manufactured from wood fibers, strands, or veneers, enhancing strength and stability for construction applications. Key types include plywood, made of thin layers glued at right angles for strength; oriented strand board (OSB), which uses strands bonded with adhesives for durability; glued laminated timber (glulam), crafted by layering wood laminates for large spans; and laminated veneer lumber (LVL), formed from multiple layers of veneers, ideal for beams and headers. These products offer design flexibility and sustainability.</p></p>
