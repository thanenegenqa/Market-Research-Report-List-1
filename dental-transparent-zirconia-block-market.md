Low translucency blocks are suitable for frameworks where strength is prioritized over aesthetics, commonly used for posterior restorations. Medium translucency blocks offer a balance between strength and aesthetics, ideal for anterior and posterior applications. High translucency blocks provide superior aesthetics, making them perfect for natural-looking anterior restorations. Each type serves specific clinical needs based on strength, appearance, and application.</p></p>
