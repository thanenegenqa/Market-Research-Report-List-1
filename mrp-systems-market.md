<p><p>MRP (Material Requirements Planning) systems are essential for managing manufacturing processes and inventory. These systems can be categorized into two market types: on-premise and on-cloud. On-premise MRP solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and security but require significant upfront costs and maintenance. In contrast, on-cloud MRP systems are hosted remotely, providing easier access and scalability with lower initial expenses. Businesses choose between these options based on their operational needs, budget, and IT infrastructure.</p></p>
