<p><p>The cardiovascular catheters and systems market is divided into two primary types: therapeutic and diagnostic catheters. </p><p>Cardiovascular therapeutic catheters are used for treatment purposes, including procedures like angioplasty, stent placement, and thrombus removal. These catheters are designed to deliver therapies directly to the cardiovascular system.</p><p>On the other hand, cardiovascular diagnostic catheters are employed for diagnostic procedures to assess heart function and blood flow. They facilitate the measurement of pressures, oxygen levels, and imaging of the heart and blood vessels, aiding in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
