<p><p>Account Data Management Software (ADMS) can be categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data and customizations but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based ADMS is hosted on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. This type is often updated automatically and offers ease of access from anywhere, making it suitable for businesses seeking efficiency and reduced IT burdens.</p></p>
