<p><p>The Rapid Antigen and Antibody Detection Kits market includes two primary types: Rapid Antigen Tests and Rapid Antibody Tests. Rapid Antigen Tests detect specific proteins from pathogens, providing quick results for active infections, making them essential for immediate diagnosis and control of outbreaks. In contrast, Rapid Antibody Tests identify antibodies in the blood, indicating past exposure to pathogens. These tests are valuable for serological surveys, helping assess population immunity and inform public health strategies. Together, they enhance rapid diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
