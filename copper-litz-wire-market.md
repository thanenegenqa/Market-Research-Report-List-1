<p><p>The Copper Litz Wire market is primarily segmented into two types: Directly Bunched Type and Multi-step Bunched Type. The Directly Bunched Type features strands of wire twisted together in a single layer, enhancing flexibility and reducing skin effect at high frequencies. In contrast, the Multi-step Bunched Type comprises multiple layers of twisted wires, providing superior performance in complex applications, including RF and induction heating. Both types aim to improve electrical efficiency and performance in various industrial and consumer electronics.</p></p>
