<p><strong>The Ultra Clear Flat Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Clear Flat Glass market primarily includes two key types: rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rolls, resulting in a smooth, uniform surface. It is commonly used in applications requiring enhanced optical clarity. Float glass, on the other hand, is manufactured by floating molten glass on molten tin, creating a flawless, flat surface. Both types offer exceptional transparency and are widely utilized in architectural, automotive, and decorative applications, driving market demand.</p></p>
