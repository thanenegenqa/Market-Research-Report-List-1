Dry Ice Therapy employs solid carbon dioxide, providing localized freezing treatment for specific areas. Electric Cryotherapy uses advanced technology to produce cold air without using cryogenic materials, delivering customizable and consistent temperature therapy. Each method serves unique therapeutic needs in recovery and wellness applications.</p></p>
