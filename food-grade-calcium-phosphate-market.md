MCP is primarily used as a nutritional supplement and leavening agent. DCP serves as a dietary supplement and a source of calcium and phosphorus in animal feed. TCP, known for its high phosphorus content, is utilized in food processing and as a ceramic additive. Each type caters to diverse applications in the food industry.</p></p>
