<p><ul><li>Sealed Lead Acid Battery</li><li>Lithium Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric moped market primarily consists of two battery types: sealed lead-acid (SLA) and lithium-ion batteries. SLA batteries are cost-effective and widely used in budget-friendly models, offering decent performance but with heavier weight and shorter lifespan. In contrast, lithium batteries are lighter, more efficient, and provide longer range and quicker charging times, making them popular in high-performance electric mopeds. The choice between these battery types influences the moped's price, performance, and overall user experience in the market.</p></p>
