<p><p>The TPU filament market is primarily divided into polyester-based and polyether-based segments. Polyester-based TPU offers excellent mechanical properties and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring durability, such as automotive parts and industrial components. In contrast, polyether-based TPU provides superior flexibility and lower temperature sensitivity, making it ideal for applications like footwear, medical devices, and soft-touch products. Both types cater to diverse industries, emphasizing performance characteristics that meet specific application requirements while ensuring compatibility with various 3D printing technologies.</p></p>
