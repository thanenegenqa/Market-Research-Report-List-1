<p><p>The TCON chip market is divided into two main types: independent TCON chips and integrated TCON chips. Independent TCON chips function as standalone components, offering flexibility and customization for various display technologies. They are commonly used in high-performance applications requiring specific timing control. In contrast, integrated TCON chips combine timing control functions with other functionalities within a single chip, providing compactness and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for mass-produced displays like smartphones and tablets. Both types cater to diverse application needs.</p></p>
