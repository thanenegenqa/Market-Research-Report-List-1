<p><p>The Hospital Linen Supply Market comprises two primary types: rental and customer-owned goods. The rental market involves healthcare facilities acquiring linens and textiles from service providers who maintain, clean, and replenish these items, ensuring consistent quality and compliance with hygiene standards.
