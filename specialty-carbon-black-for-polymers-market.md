<p><p>The Specialty Carbon Black for Polymers market includes various types, each serving unique applications. Lamp Black is produced from the incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons, offering excellent color and pigment properties. Acetylene Black, derived from the thermal decomposition of acetylene gas, is noted for its high conductivity and reinforcing qualities. Gas Black, produced through gas-phase processes, provides a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. Other types include furnace black and channel black, catering to specialized industrial needs in polymer applications.</p></p>
