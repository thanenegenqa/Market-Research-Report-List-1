<p><p>The radiation monitoring market can be categorized into X-ray, gamma ray, and beta ray segments, each serving distinct purposes. X-ray monitoring is crucial in medical settings to ensure patient and staff safety from ionizing radiation exposure during imaging procedures. Gamma ray monitoring is vital in nuclear power and medical applications to detect and measure high-energy radiation levels. Beta ray monitoring focuses on tracking lower-energy radiation emissions, essential in laboratories and industries where radioactive materials are handled, ensuring compliance with safety regulations.</p></p>
