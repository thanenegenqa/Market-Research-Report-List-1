<p><p>The electric actuator market is primarily categorized into DC electric actuators and AC electric actuators. DC electric actuators operate on direct current, offering precise control and faster response times, making them ideal for applications requiring high accuracy. Conversely, AC electric actuators utilize alternating current and are typically used in applications demanding higher power and durability. Both types serve various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, facilitating automated processes and enhancing efficiency in control systems.</p></p>
