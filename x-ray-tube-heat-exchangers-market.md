<p><p>The X-ray tube heat exchangers market is primarily divided into two types: rotating anode and stationary anode systems. Rotating anode X-ray tubes feature a continuously rotating target, allowing for better heat dissipation and improved image quality, making them ideal for high-demand applications. In contrast, stationary anode tubes have a fixed target, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness for lower intensity uses. Each type addresses specific performance needs, influencing their adoption across various medical and industrial imaging sectors.</p></p>
