<p><p>Industrial Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) can be categorized into two main types: On-Premises and On-Demand. On-Premises MES solutions are installed directly on a company's servers, providing full control over data and infrastructure but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. In contrast, On-Demand MES, often cloud-based, offers flexibility by allowing manufacturers to access systems remotely with lower initial investment and maintenance concerns. This model is increasingly popular due to its scalability and ease of integration with existing technologies.</p></p>
